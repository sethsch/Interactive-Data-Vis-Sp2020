Proactive and Reactive Government Responses to COVID-19

How long did it take governments to initiate policy responses to the pandemic?

The global COVID-19 pandemic set off a cascade of responses by governments around the world. For this project, I want to design a tool that hilights developing policy responses to COVID-19 by governments across the globe. I will draw from data from the Coronanet Research Project that's actively being collected by a team of 200+ researchers from around the world, led by NYU Abu Dhabi, TU Munich, Yale University.
